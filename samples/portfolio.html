About

We're a team of designers, developers, and strategists who believe that great design should be accessible to everyone. No exceptions. No excuses.

Founded in 2019 in Berlin, Studio Kante has worked with startups, cultural institutions, and enterprise clients across Europe and North America. Our work sits at the intersection of brutalist aesthetics and thoughtful user experience.
